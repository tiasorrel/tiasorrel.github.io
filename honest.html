The Honest Company

As the Digital Design Intern, I worked within the UX Department, as well as the larger Creative team. I am legally limited in what I am allowed to show, but I have provided explanations of the projects I worked on over the course of the internship.

Careers Section

I redesigned the careers section of the Honest website, which gets an average of 140,000 page views a year.

  • I started with researching and wireframing.
  • From there, I designed the UI to follow the website's new branding requirements.
  • I then ran user tests on the mobile versions of both the original site and my redesigns to compare their performance.
  • In tests of my careers section redesign:
    • 100% of users mentioned that it seems like employees are very cared for at this company.
    • Users had much higher levels of interest in working at Honest as compared to users viewing the original site.
    • Users felt less need to do outside research about the company on sites like Glassdoor, since they felt that this careers site gave them all the information they needed.
